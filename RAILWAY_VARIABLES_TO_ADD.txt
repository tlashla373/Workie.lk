========================================
IMPORTANT MONGODB ATLAS SETUP
========================================

Your MongoDB connection will fail unless you:

1. Go to MongoDB Atlas (https://cloud.mongodb.com)
2. Click on "Network Access" in the left sidebar
3. Click "Add IP Address"
4. Click "ALLOW ACCESS FROM ANYWHERE"
5. Enter 0.0.0.0/0
6. Click "Confirm"

This allows Railway to connect to your database.

========================================
AFTER ADDING ALL VARIABLES
========================================

1. Railway will automatically redeploy
2. Go to Deployments tab
3. Click on the new deployment
4. Click "View Logs"
5. Look for these success messages:
   - "MongoDB connected successfully"
   - "Server running in production mode on port XXXX"
6. Healthcheck should pass!
7. Get your Railway URL from Settings → Generate Domain

========================================
TEST MONGODB CONNECTION LOCALLY FIRST
========================================

Before deploying, test your MongoDB connection:

1. Open terminal in backend folder
2. Run: node test-mongo-connection.js
3. If it says "SUCCESS", your MongoDB URI is working
4. If it fails, fix the MongoDB connection before deploying
